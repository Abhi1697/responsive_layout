About

Myself

I am currently 22 years old, studied Electronics & Communication Engineering and now aspires to become a Web Developer. Ever since I was a kid computer has been my passion. I still remember printing out my name for the first time from an old HP personal computer and sitting in front of it for hours. It was almost like magic, being able to get the computer do whatever I wanted, all out of my imagination and ingenuity. Throughout my middle and high school, I would spend most of my time in the computer labs trying to acquire the most I could get. I was insatiable. This passion carried me through my education at JIS College of Engineering. More and more resources were available here than I could have imagined as a kid. I procured knowledge about programming languages, computer architectures, algorithms, higher mathematics, etc. with the same fascination that drove me as a kid. I learned C, then Python, and went all the way from just printing out "Hello, World!" to building my first ever application software. So, now, in any organisation, this passion of mine remains. There's certainly yet more to learn, yet more problems to solve and yet more things to build. And, I am beholden for this.
